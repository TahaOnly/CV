• About me

Now that I know you're interested, let me tell you more about myself. I was born in Kot Radha Kishan, Kasur. My family moved to Lahore soon after my birth.

I am a CS major at Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS). I work as the CEO of Sutoon, an NGO I started in 2016 with three close friends.

I spend a lot of time reading about Startups, Product Design, and Management, and have recently started learning more about storytelling and design thinking. I also love exploring the heritage in Lahore. Occasionally I visit Old Lahore and Mughalpura to experience the glorious Mughal architecture.

I hope that whatever work I do unites my various passions, and brings lasting impact to communities around me.

Feel free to email me at 23100098@lums.edu.pk! I love meeting with people in person, so if you're currently based in Lahore feel free to reach out for a (socially distanced) cup of coffee!
